Saving Water Tips

Simple and effective ways to conserve water

Tip 1: Turn Off the Tap

Don’t leave the water running when brushing your teeth or washing dishes. Save up to 6 liters per minute.

Tip 2: Take Shorter Showers

Reduce your shower time by just 2 minutes to save up to 20 liters of water each time.

Tip 3: Water Plants in the Morning

Watering plants early in the morning reduces evaporation and helps them absorb water better.

Tip 4: Fix Leaky Faucets

Fix leaks promptly. A dripping tap can waste more than 15 liters of water a day.

Tip 5: Reuse Water

Collect rainwater or reuse water from washing vegetables to water your garden.
