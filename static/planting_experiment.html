How to Plant a Tomato Plant

A fun and rewarding planting experiment for kids!

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

You will need tomato seeds, a small pot, soil, water, and a sunny spot.

Step 2: Fill the Pot with Soil

Fill the pot about 3/4 full with nutrient-rich soil to provide a good environment for the seeds.

Step 3: Plant the Seeds

Make small holes in the soil, about 1/4 inch deep, and place 2-3 tomato seeds in each hole. Cover lightly with soil.

Step 4: Water the Soil

Gently water the soil to make it moist but not soggy. Be careful not to overwater.

Step 5: Place in a Sunny Spot

Put the pot in a sunny location, such as a windowsill or balcony, to ensure the seeds get enough light.

Step 6: Watch Them Grow!

Within a few days to a week, you should see small sprouts. Keep watering regularly and watch your tomato plant grow!
